Our Vision

Traditional interviews are often biased, inconsistent, and stressful. Great candidates are rejected for the wrong reasons.

We believe hiring should be fair, skill-focused, and accessible. Our mission is to empower both candidates and recruiters with intelligent, unbiased interview experiences.

About Our Project

AI Interview is an intelligent interview platform designed to automate technical and behavioral interviews using artificial intelligence. The system conducts interviews via voice or text, evaluates responses in real time, and generates structured reports for HR teams.

Candidates interact with an AI interviewer in a calm, pressure-free environment, while recruiters receive objective insights based on communication skills, confidence, accuracy, and problem-solving ability.

Our platform supports secure interview sessions, cheating detection, analytics dashboards, and role-based access — making hiring faster, fairer, and scalable.
