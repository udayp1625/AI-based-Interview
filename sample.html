How it Works

Candidate Login & Verification

The candidate logs into the platform using their username and password. Once authenticated, the system prepares the interview environment.

Resume Upload & Setup

The candidate uploads their CV. The platform requests camera/mic permissions and performs a system check.

AI-Powered Interview Session

The AI interviewer presents questions. The candidate answers in real-time while the system records data for analysis.

Automated Report Generation

The AI evaluates responses, behavior, and communication. A detailed report is generated for the HR team.
